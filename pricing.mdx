## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I switch plans?" icon="arrows-rotate">
    Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately, while downgrades take effect at the end of your billing cycle.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What happens if I exceed my limits?" icon="triangle-exclamation">
    For Free and paid tiers, requests will be throttled once you hit your daily limits. With Pay As You Go, you'll continue to be charged for additional usage. You can set up alerts to notify you when approaching limits.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Do you offer discounts for nonprofits or education?" icon="graduation-cap">
    Yes! We offer 50% discounts for verified nonprofits and educational institutions. Contact us at support@tryintra.app for more information.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Is there a free trial for paid plans?" icon="clock">
    Yes! All paid plans include a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What payment methods do you accept?" icon="credit-card">
    We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover), PayPal, and wire transfers for enterprise customers.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I get a refund?" icon="money-bill-wave">
    We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all paid plans. If you're not satisfied, contact us for a full refund.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
